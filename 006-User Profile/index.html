Maxence Danet-Fauvel

Maxence Danet-Fauvel is a French actor and model. He is best known for the role of Eliott Demaury in the TV series Skam France, adaptation of Skam. He started modeling in 2015 and works for the Elite Model Management. In 2016, he joined the Actors Factory in Paris.
